Privacy Policy

How YouTube Streamer collects, uses, and protects your information.

This Privacy Policy describes how YouTube Streamer (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles information when you visit our website, create an account, or use our streaming services. This page is a general template and should be adapted with your actual practices and local legal requirements.

Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our services:

  • Account details such as name, email address, and password.
  • Billing information processed through third‑party payment providers.
  • Usage data such as pages visited, actions taken in the dashboard, and device information.
  • Content you upload for streaming (video, audio, thumbnails, and related metadata).

How we use your information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain your Streamloop account.
  • Process subscription payments and manage your plan.
  • Monitor and support your live streams and service performance.
  • Communicate important updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Improve and analyze platform performance and user experience.

Cookies and tracking technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how you interact with the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but blocking them may impact certain features.

Sharing of information

We may share information with:

  • Payment processors to complete subscription transactions.
  • Cloud and infrastructure providers that host our services.
  • Analytics and monitoring tools used to improve reliability and performance.
  • Authorities when required by applicable law or legal process.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties in exchange for money. If you engage with third‑party platforms such as YouTube, their own policies apply to those services.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Streaming media and logs may be kept for shorter periods based on storage limits and operational needs.

Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, update, or delete certain personal information, or to object to specific processing. You can contact us using the details in the “Contact us” section to make a request.

Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards that are designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. No online service can guarantee perfect security, so you should also keep your account credentials safe and unique.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will be revised when changes are posted. Your continued use of YouTube Streamer after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, you can contact us at:
Email: support@streamloop.in
