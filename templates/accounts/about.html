About - StreamLoop

Automating 24/7 YouTube streaming so creators can focus on content, not infrastructure.

Who we are

streamloop is a streaming automation platform built for creators, educators, and brands who want reliable, always‑on YouTube streams without managing complex servers, encoders, or command‑line tools.

The platform combines a modern Django backend, queue‑based processing, and cloud‑native video workflows to turn your pre‑recorded content into professional live streams.

Our mission

Make streaming infrastructure invisible

Streaming should feel like uploading a video, not configuring a broadcast studio. Our goal is to hide complexity behind a clean dashboard so you can create schedules, upload content, and go live with a few clicks.

From handling RTMP output to managing encoders and retries, the platform is designed to be reliable even when you are offline.

What you can do

Built for busy creators

  • Upload videos and loop them into 24/7 streams.
  • Create one‑day, monthly, or annual streaming plans.
  • Monitor stream status from a unified dashboard.
  • Scale to multiple simultaneous streams per account.

Whether you are streaming music, lectures, or devotional content, streamloop helps keep your channel active and your audience engaged.
